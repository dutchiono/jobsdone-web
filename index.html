Every business is different. Click on a trade to see how we’ve helped businesses like yours.

🔧

Lead-to-booked in 5 minutes

HVAC Contractor

Mike runs a 6-truck HVAC operation. He was missing calls constantly while on job sites — and every missed call was a lost customer. Leads would come in through Google, sit in his inbox for hours, and by the time he called back, they’d already booked someone else.

We set up an AI agent that responds to every inquiry within 60 seconds, asks a few qualifying questions, and automatically books the estimate on his calendar. Now Mike shows up to jobs that are already confirmed — no phone tag, no missed leads.

The Result

Lead response time dropped from 4+ hours to under 1 minute. Booked estimates increased 34% in the first 60 days. Mike stopped losing jobs to faster competitors.

🌿

Customer reviews on autopilot

Lawn Care

Sarah runs a lawn care business with 4 crews. She knew reviews were important for showing up in local search, but she never remembered to ask customers. And when she did ask, most people forgot — she was lucky to get 1 review a month.

We automated the entire process. Now when a job is marked complete in FieldFlow, the customer automatically gets a text thanking them and asking for a Google review. No effort from Sarah, no awkward conversations, just a steady stream of reviews.

The Result

47 reviews in 3 months. Her Google Business listing jumped from page 3 to the top of local results. She now gets 3x more calls from Google than she did before.

⚡

Job dashboard, no more spreadsheets

Electrician

Derek runs a 9-person electrical company. He was tracking jobs in a spreadsheet, invoices in QuickBooks, and customer notes in a notebook. Nothing talked to each other, and he was constantly losing track of where things stood.

We set him up on FieldFlow with a custom dashboard that shows all open jobs, pending invoices, and customer history in one view. Now he pulls out his phone and knows exactly what’s happening — who’s working, what’s due, and who needs a callback.

The Result

Derek spends 2 hours less per week on admin work. He caught $3,200 in unbilled work in the first month just by having visibility into his jobs.

🚰

Social posted without thinking about it

Plumber

Carlos knows social media is important, but he’s busy running calls all day. He’d take photos of finished jobs and they’d sit on his phone for weeks. By the time he got around to posting, the moment was gone — and he never knew what to write anyway.

We made it stupid simple. Carlos finishes a job, snaps a photo, texts it to us. We write the caption and post it to Facebook, Instagram, and his Google Business profile. He does zero work and his social presence stays active.

The Result

12 posts per month across 3 platforms. His Google Business engagement is up 4x. Customers mention seeing his work on Facebook all the time.

🧹

Recurring payment, zero invoicing

Cleaning Company

Jennifer runs a residential cleaning company with 45 recurring clients. Every month she’d spend a full day creating invoices, sending them out, and then chasing payments. Half her clients paid late. Some never paid at all.

We moved all her recurring clients to auto-pay through FieldFlow. Now invoices are generated automatically, cards are charged automatically, and if a payment fails, the system sends a reminder and retries. Jennifer doesn’t touch any of it.

The Result

Collections time dropped from 8 hours/month to zero. On-time payments went from 55% to 98%. Jennifer stopped chasing checks entirely.

🏭

Estimate tool on the website

Roofer

Tony gets a lot of tire-kickers. People want to know what a new roof costs, but they don’t want to commit to an in-person estimate. He was spending hours driving to appointments that never converted.

We built an estimate calculator right into his website. Customers put in their square footage, roof type, and a few details. They get an instant ballpark range. If they like what they see, they book the in-person estimate right there — and Tony shows up to a warm lead with all the info ready.

The Result

Estimate conversion rate jumped from 30% to 65%. Tony spends less time on unqualified leads and more time closing jobs that are ready to buy.
