## Automation Blueprint

### Lead Flow
1. Customer fills out contact form on website
2. CRM records the lead
3. Automated text + email sent within 60 seconds
4. No response in 24h: follow-up text sent automatically
5. No response in 72h: email follow-up sent

### Review Flow
1. Job marked complete
2. 2 hours later: text sent asking for Google review (with direct link)
3. No review in 5 days: second text sent
4. Review posted -> flagged for social content

### Social Flow
1. Client sends photo via text or email
2. Auto-route to content queue
3. Caption written (AI-assisted)
4. Scheduled and posted to all platforms

### Onboarding Flow
1. Client signs up (emails jobsdoneteam@gmail.com)
2. Onboarding form sent — business info, photos, service area
3. Build begins: website -> Google Business -> social profiles
4. Review call at 48 hours
5. Client approves -> go live
6. Monthly check-in scheduled
