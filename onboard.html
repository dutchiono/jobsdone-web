Common questions

How fast do you get things set up?

The Starter plan is typically live within 48 hours. Growth and Done For You take 3–5 business days for full setup. Custom builds depend on scope — we'll give you a clear timeline upfront.

Do I need to be tech-savvy?

No. That's the whole point. You run your business — we handle the tech. We'll explain what we're building and why, but you never have to touch any of it.

What kinds of businesses do you work with?

Mostly local service businesses — HVAC, plumbing, electrical, lawn care, cleaning, roofing, painting, and similar trades. If you're a business that does work in the field, we're built for you.

Is there a contract?

No long-term contracts. Month-to-month on all plans. We keep clients because the work is good — not because they're locked in.

What's OpenClaw and do I need it?

OpenClaw is a popular open-source business platform. We install and configure it when it's the right fit. But we often build lighter custom solutions that are simpler to run and cheaper to maintain. We'll recommend what actually makes sense for you.
